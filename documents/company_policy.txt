# TechVision Inc - Company Policy & FAQ

## Refund Policy
All digital products have a 30-day money-back guarantee.
Physical products can be returned within 14 days if unopened.
To request a refund, email support@techvision.com with order number.

## Pricing
- Starter Plan: $49/month (up to 5 users)
- Professional Plan: $149/month (up to 25 users)
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing (unlimited users)
Annual billing gives 20% discount on all plans.

## Support Hours
Support team available Monday-Friday, 9AM-6PM EST.
Emergency support for Enterprise clients: 24/7.
Average response time: under 2 hours.

## Product Features
Core features include: dashboard analytics, API access, 
team collaboration tools, and automated reporting.
Enterprise features add: SSO, custom integrations, 
dedicated account manager, and SLA guarantee.
