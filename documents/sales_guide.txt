# TechVision Inc - Sales Playbook 2024

## Company Mission
Empowering businesses with AI-driven insights to make faster, smarter decisions.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
### Primary ICP
- B2B SaaS companies with 50-500 employees
- Annual revenue $5M-$100M
- Currently using 3+ disconnected analytics tools
- Pain points: slow reporting, data silos, manual work, lack of real-time insights

### Secondary ICP
- E-commerce companies processing 1000+ orders/day
- Financial services firms needing compliance reporting
- Healthcare organizations requiring HIPAA-compliant analytics

## Value Proposition
1. Time Savings: Average customer saves 15 hours/week on manual reporting
2. Cost Reduction: Replace 3-4 separate tools with one platform (avg savings $2,000/month)
3. Revenue Impact: Customers report 23% increase in sales productivity
4. Risk Reduction: Real-time alerts prevent issues before they escalate

## Competitor Analysis
### vs Tableau
- We win on: pricing (40% cheaper), ease of use, AI features
- They win on: visualization depth, brand recognition
- Counter: "Tableau requires a dedicated analyst. TechVision works for your whole team."

### vs Looker (Google)
- We win on: implementation speed (days vs months), customer support, pricing
- They win on: Google ecosystem integration
- Counter: "Looker takes 3-6 months to implement. We're live in 48 hours."

### vs Power BI
- We win on: AI capabilities, mobile experience, customer success
- They win on: Microsoft integration, price for existing Microsoft customers
- Counter: "Power BI requires significant IT resources. TechVision is self-serve."

## Objection Handling
### "It's too expensive"
Response: "Let's calculate your ROI. If your team saves 15 hours/week at $50/hour, that's $3,000/month in productivity gains against our $149/month plan. That's 20x ROI."

### "We already have a solution"
Response: "What does your current reporting process look like? Most of our customers came from [competitor] because they needed [specific feature]. Can I show you how we solve that?"

### "Not the right time"
Response: "I understand. What would need to change for this to become a priority? Is it budget cycle, team bandwidth, or something else?"

### "Need to think about it"
Response: "Absolutely. What specific questions do you still have? I'd like to make sure you have everything you need to make the best decision."

## Sales Process
1. Discovery Call (30 min): Understand pain points, current tools, team size
2. Demo (45 min): Personalized demo focused on their specific use case
3. Proof of Concept (14 days): Free trial with real data
4. Proposal: Custom pricing based on users and features needed
5. Negotiation: Maximum 15% discount without VP approval
6. Close: Always get next step commitment before ending any meeting

## Closing Techniques
- Always end calls with a specific next step and date
- Send follow-up email within 1 hour of any meeting
- Use case studies from same industry as prospect
- Create urgency with end-of-quarter pricing
- Executive sponsorship for deals over $50K

## Customer Success Metrics

- Customer satisfaction (CSAT): 4.8/5
- Annual churn rate: 4.2% (industry average: 8%)
