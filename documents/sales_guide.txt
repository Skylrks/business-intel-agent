# TechVision Inc - Sales Playbook

## Ideal Customer Profile
- B2B SaaS companies with 10-200 employees
- Annual revenue $1M-$50M
- Currently using manual spreadsheet processes
- Pain points: slow reporting, data silos, manual work

## Objection Handling
Price too high: Emphasize ROI - average client saves 15 hours/week
Not the right time: Ask what needs to change for timing to be right
Need to think about it: Offer free 14-day trial, no credit card needed

## Competitor Comparison
vs Competitor A: We have better API, they have lower price
vs Competitor B: We have more integrations, they have older UI
Our unique advantage: Only platform with built-in AI automation

## Closing Techniques
Always end calls with a clear next step.
Send follow-up email within 1 hour of any meeting.
Use case studies from same industry as prospect.
